# Recommended Books for Interpretation Experiment

Ideas for knowledge bases to index in Qdrant for dream interpretation. Different lenses yield different readings of the same dream content.

| Book | Author | Angle |
|------|--------|-------|
| Man and His Symbols | Carl Jung | Archetypes, dream symbols, classical interpretation |
| The Dreaming Brain | J. Allan Hobson | Neuroscience of sleep, REM, activation-synthesis |
| Why We Sleep | Matthew Walker | Sleep science, memory, metabolism |
| Lucid Dreaming | Stephen LaBerge | Lucid dreaming research, techniques |
| The Ego Tunnel | Thomas Metzinger | Consciousness, phenomenal self-model |
| Surfing Uncertainty | Andy Clark | Predictive processing, dreams as simulation |
| Incognito | David Eagleman | Unconscious processing, brain |
| Superintelligence | Nick Bostrom | Simulation argument, AI, consciousness limits |
| VALIS / The Exegesis | Philip K. Dick | Simulation, reality, gnostic themes |
| The Paradox of Sleep | Michel Jouvet | REM, paradoxical sleep, neurophysiology |

**By focus:**
- **Interpretation:** Jung, LaBerge
- **Neuroscience:** Hobson, Walker, Jouvet, Eagleman
- **Consciousness:** Metzinger, Clark
- **Simulation:** Bostrom, Dick
