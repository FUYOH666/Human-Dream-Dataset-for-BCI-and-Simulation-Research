# Dream Catalog

~50 reports, 80–90 distinct episodes. Anonymized, structured by narrative motifs.

---

## Episodes 1–20

1. **Quest between worlds:** Lucid person, passage through wall/shadow corridor, another world underwater, chance to surface in different layer, helping man with "harmful dog", scene with people and story about real estate/karma.

2. **Old friend and AI-agent pitch:** Acquaintance introduces you to influential person from entertainment industry, you sell idea of AI-agent for communications, idea is well received.

3. **Multi-episode strange dream:** Harmful cat, nervous guy among acquaintances, drowned cat underwater, people on stairs and fall from above.

4. **Dream about justice:** Person who once cheated you tries to "repay debt" with junk and disks instead of money.

5. **Big store and aggressive type:** Near-fight, but you don't start it; second fragment — phrase "what if this is reality and in the morning you return to the dream?"

6. **Another woman and distant country:** Meeting, journey, electric bike, old houses for demolition, sense of global route and future encounter.

7. **Two dreams in a row:**
   - Car, ferry, turtle, people eating your yogurt, coordination falling apart;
   - Small plane landing, broken store window, insurance, you are very rich and calm.

8. **Abandoned car and strange district:** Incorrectly registered car, detour through puddle, then noisy neighbors turn into hiding criminals in the dream.

9. **Spontaneous trip and missed flight:** Another country, little money, water slide instead of escalator, non-refundable ticket, crypto; separate fragment — obsessive encounter.

10. **Contact from social media and trip to unknown city:** Someone writes "we know each other", invites you, you try to find meaning and money for ticket, but goal is unclear.

11. **New woman in the house:** Closing windows and doors, preparing to leave, talking with her about ex and old acquaintance story.

12. **Underground council:** Dark auditorium/dungeon, huge oak table, serious men 40+, someone wants to rewrite rules and sit at the head.

13. **Criminal group and New Year:** You among people of "shadow world", they trust you, you help, wish them love and happiness and calmly leave.

14. **Lush but empty wedding:** Rich bride's father, groom as if against his will, relative's grave, seaside pier, many people and communities, but celebration feels lifeless.

15. **Another country and real estate:** Hype on buying apartments, you explain to acquaintances how to bypass useless agent, choosing between finished and under construction, thinking about commercial spaces and apartments worldwide.

16. **Fish factory:** Giant fish production, vertical logistics, funicular, three cars, you buy SUV at good price.

17. **White villa by water:** Old acquaintance built villa, huge residential complex grew opposite and ruined the view, you discuss sale, romantic episode there too.

18. **Mountain alien world:** Old friend stuck in cycle, possible ex, exchanging large speaker for compact one, then closed industrial territory and forest road to brilliant engineer, strange forest party/brain reboot.

19. **Elevator and high floors:** New building, elevator as attraction, 8th and 24th floor, fear of speed and height, then huge rich structure building ships.

20. **Underwater spy mission:** Meeting point, diving gear, mask choice, underwater channels, partner taken by the system, you choose mission and exit through non-obvious channel.

## Episodes 21–40

21. **Islands, road and social engineering:** Suspiciously burst tire, overly helpful old man, attempt to get QR/account access, rescue through independent communication channel.

22. **Boats between islands:** New incompetent employee of ferry company, wrong routes, sense of fake importance of intermediaries.

23. **"Level game" and 25/30 repetitions:** Business as game with cycles, military figure, boundary violation, murder as jump to level 28–29 instead of passing from zero.

24. **Expectations and distorted results / red light / substances:** People's expectations change outcome; youth drives to graduation, runs red light, mixes substances; then theme of license/documents renewal.

25. **Adults sliding "penguin-style" on connected tables:** Everything supported by props, pets well-kept and watching.

26. **Approach to castle:** Mountain serpentine, huge castle on green slope; sense that "penguin" dream happened there.

27. **Summit on attention control via AI:** "Firmware" for phone, countries discussing attention manipulation, then armed conflict, evacuation, error in decision and self-destructive character finale.

28. **Preamble to same plot:** Partially disassembled vehicle, old friends, talks about "custom AI firmware", mirrored phone.

29. **Old wooden house:** Old friends, broken toilet on first floor, another toilet upstairs, old phone with many extra tabs, taxi choice between two locations.

30. **Science town and repeating fun:** With old friends you relive same cheerful scenes from different viewpoints, thanking each other for mutual support.

31. **Relative's apartment as white closed ecosystem:** Three spacious white rooms, much wildlife that's ok by itself, but system doesn't self-clean and requires endless maintenance.

32. **Grade book / thesis / medical history:** Long wait for signature, strange mix of exam and medical recommendations, plus vague idea of party on someone else's villa.

33. **Forest camp and drone:** Camping in wilderness, quadcopter as reconnaissance and weather tool, move to other side of forest, "hut" with internet, speakers, TV and fridge, plane as way out.

34. **Stellar/future landing:** Landing with 6 or 12 kg gear.

35. **Server/product and two-story building:** You deploy something on server, team nearby, outside people carrying 70-inch TV like smartphone, among them old acquaintance.

36. **Waterfront and glances:** Guy and girl exchange glances, then you also meet her gaze — and that's it.

37. **AI dreams:**
   - Model Qwen 3.5 / A3B, study/trial;
   - Another AI fragment;
   - Main: flight to Asian city, pitch of three ideas — radiology, customs copilot, marketplace copilot.

38. **Twins:** Cute twin girls taking turns telling you mundane things; before that was car ride.

39. **Rich man, road and "house-hotel":** Red traffic lights, strangely large property object; then action with hero resembling movie star, guns and meteor shower.

40. **Distorted versions of events after drinking:** Old friend allegedly offended woman, but everyone remembers differently; then treasure room/warehouse with statues and figures, then old bicycle and stadium where they play "not your sport".

57. **Old house / gathering / broken toilet:** Same as #29, separately recalled and confirmed details.

59. **Old friends from science town and warm togetherness:** Same as #30, separately emphasized as very kind, grateful dream.

60. **Relatives scenario of closed ecosystem:** Same as #31, returned to as very important metaphor.
